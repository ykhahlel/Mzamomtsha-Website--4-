EVENTS AT

MZAMONTSHA PRIMARY SCHOOL

We are very excited to announce that we have a number of exciting events planned for the year

Awards Day event:

This years sports day will take place on Thursday, 9th June and will be run by our PE teacher, Mrs Celi. It will be a great opportunity for our children to take part in a variety of fun, competitive team games such as handball, sack races and egg-and-spoon relays, as well as enjoy a picnic lunch and games of rounders outside on the field. The sports day will be followed by a party in the school hall for all the children and staff to enjoy. All parents are very welcome to attend this event. We are hoping that lots of you will come and support your child at this event and join in the fun with us!
